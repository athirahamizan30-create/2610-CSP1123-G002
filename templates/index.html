Introduction to
CareerTrack

The process of finding a job could be a bit difficult to handle for some people.
people might lose track of the jobs that they have already applied for,
which may cause them to lose the opportunities that they supposed to grab.
Therefore, a web-based application called
CareerTrack to help users to track their application status,
record important details of the jobs and organise essential documents.
This system also includes the ability to set the important dates and also categorise
the job based on the type of employment.
